SECTION 8: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)
================================================================================

Q: What exactly is an AI Agent?
A: An AI Agent is a custom-built intelligent system that automates specific
business tasks. Unlike simple chatbots, our agents can make decisions, learn
from interactions, integrate with your existing tools, and handle complex
multi-step workflows autonomously.

Q: How long does implementation take?
A: Typical implementation takes 4-8 weeks:
- Week 1-2: Discovery and strategy alignment
- Week 2-4: Custom development and design
- Week 5: Onboarding and training
- Week 6+: Integration, optimization, and scaling

Q: Do I need technical expertise to use Arkadia Labs?
A: No. We handle all the technical complexity. Your team just needs to
participate in the discovery phase and training sessions. After that, the
agents work autonomously with your existing tools.

Q: What if my tools aren't on your integration list?
A: We can build custom integrations for most systems. If you have internal
tools or proprietary software, we'll work with your team to create the
necessary connections.

Q: How do you ensure data security?
A: We are a Swiss company following strict Swiss data protection standards
(which exceed GDPR). All data is encrypted, we don't store sensitive
information unnecessarily, and we're fully GDPR and CCPA compliant.

Q: What ROI can I expect?
A: Based on our case studies:
- Goodnite achieved 6x ROI in 12 months
- Domus Labs achieved 160% ROI in 3 months
Typical clients see positive ROI within 3-6 months.

Q: Can I start small and scale later?
A: Absolutely! Many clients start with our AI Employee plan (1 agent) to
validate results, then scale to AI Sales Team or AI Growth Partner as they
see ROI.

Q: What happens if the AI makes a mistake?
A: Our agents are designed with human oversight. You can set approval
workflows for sensitive actions. Plus, our optimization sessions help us
continuously improve accuracy based on real-world performance.

Q: Do you offer a trial or demo?
A: Yes! You can:
1. Talk to our AI voice agent on the website (demo)
2. Book a free 15-minute consultation call
3. Request a custom demo for your specific use case

Q: What industries do you serve?
A: We serve multiple industries including:

- SaaS & Technology
- Professional Services
- Manufacturing

Q: How is Arkadia Labs different from other AI solutions?
A: Key differences:
1. We build custom agents, not generic templates
2. Swiss precision and quality standards

4. Ongoing optimization partnership
