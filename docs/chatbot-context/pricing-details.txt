================================================================================
FREQUENTLY ASKED PRICING QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Is this a monthly subscription?
A: No. The price shown is a one-time setup fee. After setup, ongoing strategy
calls, support, and optimizations are included in your plan.

Q: What external tools might I need to pay for?
A: Depending on your use case:
- Twilio (for voice/SMS): Pay as you go
- CRM subscription (if you don't have one)
- Third-party APIs specific to your integrations
We'll tell you exactly what you need during discovery.

Q: Can I upgrade my plan later?
A: Yes! Many clients start with AI Employee to validate results, then upgrade
to AI Sales Team or AI Growth Partner. You only pay the difference.

Q: Do you offer custom pricing for specific needs?
A: Yes. For unique requirements or enterprise deals, contact our sales team
for a custom quote.

Q: What's included in the setup fee?
A: Discovery, design, development, integration, testing, training, and launch
of your AI system(s). Plus ongoing support and optimization.

Q: How does the 20% annual discount work?
A: Contact sales for annual pricing. This typically applies to companies
wanting extended partnership terms.

Q: Are there any hidden fees?
A: No. The only additional costs are external tools you choose to use
(and those are paid directly to the tool providers, not to us).

Q: What if I need more than 10 AI systems?
A: Contact our sales team for enterprise pricing. We've handled larger
deployments and can create a custom package.
